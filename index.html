Welcome to Towards Digital Collections, Resources for Galleries, Libraries, Archives and Museums

This is a holding page which is currently under development and will be used to signpost to key information and resources being developed.

The Towards Digital Collections, Resources for Galleries, Libraries, Archives and Museums training course is being developed by Ex Nihilo in collaboration with the University of Oxford (further information here), commissioned by the Towards a National Collection Programme - a major five-year £18.9 million investment in the UK’s world-renowned museums, archives, libraries and galleries. Funded through the UK Research and Innovation’s Strategic Priorities Fund and delivered by the Arts and Humanities Research Council (AHRC).

The training course and resources will be launched at the Towards a National Collection Two-Day Conference 2024 (20-21 November 2024).

If you’d like to find out more please contact hello@exnihiloconsulting.co.uk
